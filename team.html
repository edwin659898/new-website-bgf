MANAGEMENT TEAM

MANAGEMENT TEAM

Jean-Paul Deprins

Managing Director

Jean-Paul, hailing from Belgium, has dedicated twenty years to his role as the Managing Director of Better Globe Forestry Limited in East Africa. He brings a wealth of expertise from his extensive senior management experience in both European and East...

African contexts. Jean-Paul is a staunch proponent of ethical and transparent business practices, highlighting the importance of a systematic and evidence-driven approach to addressing challenges. He is also acutely attuned to the social issues intertwined with forestry in the East African drylands, emphasizing the need for resourcefulness and creativity in overcoming anticipated and unforeseen obstacles associated with pioneering forestry in this region.


Jan Vandenabeele

Senior Director Forestry

Jan is a Belgian forester and agronomist with 40 years of experience in management and technical implementation of locally and internationally financed projects in Europe, Africa, South America, and Asia. As a senior director...

in BGF his wealth of experience and his high level contacts in the forestry world are invaluable and have opened many doors such allowing us to be the pioneers in dryland afforestation in East Africa. As the Editor-in-chief of Miti, he is the driving force behind the magazine with editorial boards in Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania.


Lawrence Mulandi

Finance and Operations Director

Lawrence started working with Better Globe Forestry (BGF) as an accountant, became a finance manager and was later promoted to Finance Director. He has been instrumental in guiding the accounts department through the...

transition from a small company to a fast-growing medium-sized organisation. He holds a master's degree in business administration - strategic management and is now pursuing a PhD in business administration, specialising in finance. He is also a certified public accountant of Kenya - CPA (K) - and a certified internal systems auditor. Lawrence is an observer to the board.


Samuel Nakhone

Executive Director Forestry

Dedicated and results-oriented forestry professional with a passion for sustainable resource management. Adept at leveraging extensive knowledge of global forestry practices, environmental...

conservation, and strategic planning to contribute effectively to the mission of Better Glob Forestry. Over ten years of proven ability to collaborate with diverse stakeholders, implement innovative solutions, and drive positive change within the forestry sector.


Claudiah Caroline Deprins

Deputy Director Communications

Claudiah joined the Better Globe family in 2011 contributing her expertise in Child Africa as a Partnerships Coordinator. In 2018 she started working with BGF's Publication Miti Magazine as the Subscription Coordinator and assumed the role of Communications Manager...

in 2019. With an extensive track record spanning over a decade in the field of Public Relations, she became BGF's Deputy Director of Communications in 2022. Claudiah brings a wealth of experience and innovative strategies to her current position.


Lydia Kathure Kubai

Deputy Director Human Resources

Lydia joined Better Globe Forestry in the year 2018 as a Senior Accountant. She is now in charge of the Human Resources department. She holds bachelor's degree in Commerce, Finance Option and is a Certified Public...

Accountant CPA (K) with over 10 years working experience in the private sector. Lydia is a Member of the Institute of Certified Public Accounts of Kenya and is currently pursuing certification in Human Resources
