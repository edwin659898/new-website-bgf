About Us

    ABOUT Better Globe Forestry

    Better Globe Forestry plays a pivotal role in sustaining ecosystems, especially in drylands, by promoting the planting of Melia volkensii and Melia azedarach trees in collaboration with farmers or in designated woodlots. These Melia trees act as a natural shield against desertification, employing robust root systems to anchor soil, prevent erosion, and maintain environmental equilibrium. In arid regions, they function as guardians of water resources, capturing and retaining moisture to replenish groundwater levels and support a diverse range of flora and fauna.

    Better Globe Forestry - Planting Trees, Changing Lives.

    About us - Better Globe Forestry.

    Welcome to Better Globe Forestry, your trusted partner in sustainable forestry and environmental conservation. We are a forward-thinking company with a passionate commitment to making a positive impact on the world. Our journey began in 2004 when Better Globe Forestry Ltd. (BGF) was founded, with a steadfast focus on sustainable forestry practices and improving the well-being of local communities. Since then, we have expanded our operations to Uganda in 2009, with expertise in implementing large-scale sustainable forestry and agro-forestry projects, especially in arid regions.

    The Better Globe Forestry Foundation

    In 2016, in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya, the Better Globe Forestry Foundation was formally established. Its mission is two-fold: to empower smallholder farmers and their communities towards sustainability and to contribute to the preservation and enhancement of their environment. This dual objective benefits not only the local population but also the entire nation.

    Our Commitment to Reforestation.

    At Better Globe Forestry, we firmly believe in the transformative power of trees. Over the years, we have planted millions of trees across East Africa, primarily in Kenya and Uganda. These trees are carefully selected and cultivated to restore degraded land, combat climate change, and provide a sustainable source of income for local communities. Our approach to reforestation goes beyond planting trees; it involves nurturing and maintaining them to ensure long-term success. We are proud to be leaders in sustainable forestry practices, emphasizing the use of indigenous species and holistic land management techniques.

    Social Responsibility and Community Development

    True sustainability is inseparable from social responsibility. Our unwavering commitment to empowering communities near our forestry projects extends to job creation, education initiatives, access to microfinance, and training in water management. We strive to enhance the quality of life for those living in the regions where we operate, creating a positive ripple effect that extends beyond the trees we plant.

    Our Vision and Foundation

    Our overarching vision is to eradicate poverty in Africa through forestry development. We pursue this noble goal through the Better Globe Forestry Foundation, where we support farmers with tree donations, access to micro-credit, and water supply projects. These initiatives uplift local communities and foster self-reliance.
